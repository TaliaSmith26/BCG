Our Services




Our goal at Boston Community Gardens is to help the community. There are many ways we do this through our organization, but here are just a few:




We bring communities together through the shared bonding experience of gardening in the beautiful Boston area.

We provide the low-income community more access to fresh foods that we produce, hopefully leading to better dietary habits.

We improve Boston's soil and air quality, biodiversity with plants and animals, and decrease neighborhoud waste through composting.







Anybody in the Boston area is welcome to become a member. We are always thrilled to expand our gardening community!

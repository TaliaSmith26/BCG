Boston Community Gardens










At Boston Community Gardens (BCG), we are a group of Boston residents working with the Boston community and others in order to improve the quality of live for people amd strengthen community bonds. We are a nonprofit organization who practices green and sustainable gardening practices. At BCG, people create social ties while also benefiting the urban environment one plant at a time. Through our garden space, we believe we are reducing food desserts in Boston and allowing more low-income residents to have access to healthy foods.

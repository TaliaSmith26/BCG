Boston Community Gardens










At Boston Community Gardens (BCG), we are a group of Boston residents working with the Boston community and others in order to improve the quality of life for people and strengthen community bonds. Through our non-profit organization, people create social ties while also benefiting the urban environment one plant at a time. With our garden space, we are diminishing food deserts in Boston and providing under-resourced areas with more access to healthy foods. BCG is a simple way to begin a sustainable and equitable future for our community.
